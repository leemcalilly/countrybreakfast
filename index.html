The Wild Turkeys Made for your dee-vice.

Written and recorded by The Wild Turkeys at the Bomb Shelter in Nashville, TN from June 23 to 25, 2017. Engineered and mixed by Billy Bennett. Produced by The Wild Turkeys with Billy Bennett. Additional percussion and vocals by Kendra Kilkuskie. Background vocals on "Country Breakfast" by Kathleen Turchi and Liz Earle.

Advance Praise for The Wild Turkeys

"I listened to this album three times straight through in my backyard about a month ago. In the time since all of my rhododendrons and boxwoods have died. The leaves have fallen off my hardwood trees and the grass has gone dormant except for a thick line of green grass that spells out the words WHY DONT THEY START WHITE TAIL DEER AND DOVE SEASONS AT THE SAME TIME . Listener beware. I have huge landscaping remuneration bills that are coming out of pocket."

"Listening to this album gave me the same uneasy feeling as seeing someone in street clothes on a dead-run in the middle of the day. Obviously there is something wrong. Not with the music, per se, but just the fact that this exists is unsettling. At the same time I know it's not even my business to solve this problem in the first place. Why am internalizing something that has absolutely no effect on me?"

"This record is the sonic equivalent of eating a sausage biscuit from McDonalds. Take that how you will."

"Producing wunderkind Billy Bennett calls out "are we ready?" in the preface to the second track on this record. I can decidedly speak for the entire listening public with the response: "No we are not."
